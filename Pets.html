Benny

Bio: Our newest (and most energetic) resident is sweet Benny. His bark is quiet, although he uses it often. He's very well trained, and once you get to know him, he'll sit, shake hands, and when you say sick 'em, he'll slobber on the face of whoever you point at. Before you take this little guy home, make sure you're ready for like a million walks a day. And make sure to hide your treats; he'll steal anything he can see.
Immunization: Y
Age: 2 years, 1 month
Weight: 10 pounds
Price: $65
Favorite Game: Tug of war
